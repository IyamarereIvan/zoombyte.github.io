About Us

Zoombyte is a company that is committed to creating content and building platforms aimed at equipping young men and women across Africa with skills that broaden their horizon, and attracting potential investors and giving exposure to young people who are using their skills and talents to impact other people’s lives.

Services

What We Do.

Zoombyte is a film production company based in Rwanda that uses cinema to impact people’s lives. We produce content such as Film documentaries, TV Shows, poems, short films and many more to inspire, entertain and change people’s lives.

TV Shows

Zoombyte produces TV shows to help young people grow their reach for greater impact such as “Lux Series,” a show aired on TV1 featuring diverse stories of young men and women around Africa, working to impact their communities positively.

Talks

We organize various talks such as the “Cine Talk” events, meant to connect young men and women with experts in various sectors in the creative industry discussing and networking on initiatives that contribute to the growth and development of the industry.

Cine Talk: Cine Talk is a must-attend event that allows junior filmmakers to meet with senior filmmakers. The talk also unveils new projects and provides workshops for young people in various aspects of filmmaking.

Documentaries

We produce documentaries to inspire, entertain and change people’s lives such as the Kera Habayeho docu-series.

poetry videos Production

We work with the poets and provide them with high quality creative poetry videos that allow them to share their work and message with their audience..
